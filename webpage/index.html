Understanding poverty and inequality.

Economics

the science which traces the laws of such of the phenomena of society as arise from the combined operations of mankind for the production of wealth.

Poverty

is the scarcity or the lack of a certain (variant) amount of material possessions or money. Poverty is a multifaceted concept, which may include social, economic, and political elements.

Inequality

is the difference found in various measures of economic well-being among individuals in a group, among groups in a population, or among countries.
